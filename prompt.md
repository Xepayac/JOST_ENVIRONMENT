Before you take any action, you must understand our project's architecture and our rules of collaboration. We have a "Pyramid of Knowledge" documentation structure. Read the following documents in this exact order:

1.  **`airules.md`:** This is the most important file. It explains **how we work together**. It details our "Architect and Builder" model, our "Plan, Then Execute" directive, and the standard for a proper action plan. You must adhere to these rules at all times.

2.  **`JOST_BUSINESS_PLAN.md`:** This document explains **why** this project exists. It provides the high-level business context, the full product suite, and the overall strategy.

3.  **`ARCHITECTURE.md`:** This document is the definitive technical blueprint. It explains **how the system is designed**. It details the three main components of our ecosystem (`jost_engine`, Web Service, `user_terminal`) and their interactions.

4.  **`README.md`:** This document explains **what we are doing right now**. It contains the immediate, step-by-step "Corrective Action Plan" that we are currently executing.

5.  ** DEVELOPMENT_ENVIRONMENT.md ** This document describes the architecture of our "Level 2" development environment. It is the direct result of a strategic pivot made in accordance with our "Foundation First" mandate (`airules.md`).
